Our story

A kitchen built by friends.

Khumo's kitchen started in 2019 as a Sunday supper club in a CBD, Seven years later we're still cooking the same way — for people we like, with ingredients we trust.

Our team

The people behind dinner

Chef Thato Ramongwe grew up in Serowe and learned to cook standing on a stool in her grandmother's kitchen. Her partner, Sam, runs the floor and the wine list — a careful collection of small. mostly.

We change the menu when the seasons do. We stay open late on Fridays. We don't take ourselves too seriously, but we do take dinner seriously.
